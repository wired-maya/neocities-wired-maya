Hajime Advanced Robotics

Country of Origin: Japan

Motto: 未来のハジメ(Beginning of the Future)

Inspiration(s): Toha Heavy Industries (Blame!)


The world leader in robotics, invented plugs and made the first ever platforms. Recently they have been resting on their laurels and by just refining their products while new and daring ideas are pushed by foreign companies. Despite this, they hold the most market share, bolstered by their numerous military deals and the cheap reliability of their parts. They are currently attempting a hostile takeover of Nishitani Medical Technology via share buyouts as all attempts to purchase them outright have failed.
