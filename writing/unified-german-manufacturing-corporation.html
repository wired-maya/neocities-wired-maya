Unified German Manufacturing Corporation

Country of Origin: Germany

Motto: N/A

Inspiration(s): FEISAR (Wipeout), GMC


The UGMC is a megacorporation that has recently become the biggest corporation in Germany by buying out all but the most niche competition outright. Because of this, they have become responsible for 73% of the country’s GDP. They are renowned for extremely well made and robust parts in many verticals including robotics and platform technology, however due to their many investments and recent entry into the platform industry, their part releases are few and far between. Recent investment calls have shown extremely promising prototypes that could rival even the technological prowess of Hajime, however it remains to be seen if such a large and unspecialized corporation can deliver in such a fast evolving space.
