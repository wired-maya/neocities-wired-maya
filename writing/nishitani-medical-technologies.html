Nishitani Medical Technologies

Country of Origin: Japan

Motto: N/A

Inspiration(s): Medical Mechanica (FLCL), Japanese family crests


NMT specializes in making medical technology of all kinds, such as fMRI machines, automated surgery bots, and so on. They have recently broken out into the cyborg scene where they provide neuron interfaces, general implants, and a handful of mechanical eyes. Due to the reliability of their neuron interfaces and previously strong business relationship, Hajime Advanced Robotics has started sourcing parts from Nishitani.
